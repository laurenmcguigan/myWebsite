Volunteer Experience




Charity Kickball Game

The University of Pittsburgh at Greensburgh hosted their annual charity game to raise
money for Child's Play. Members of the Performing Arts Society dressed up as superhero's
for the superhero-themed event. I also put together a donation basket for the charity raffle.




Broadway Battles Breast Cancer

  • Created promotional materials to recruit performers
  • Budgeted, purchased, and created items for promotional Bake Sale
  • Performed at Event
  • Worked with companies in the area to create gift baskets for event raffle
  • Raised more that $300 for the Breast Cancer Research Foundation



Haunted Acres Manor

Members of the Performing Arts Society and Delta Psi Omega (Theatre Honor Society)
volunteered as actors for the Haunted House located in Bethel Park, PA.




SOS Animal Rescue

I planned and ran an event at The University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg where students
were able to visit and donate to adoptable cats from a local animal shelter called SOS Animal Rescue
I advertised the event and received over $200 in monetary donations as well as
other donations for the shelter.
